About Me

Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Cognitive Science Department at Prestigious University, working in the Decision Neuroscience Lab under the supervision of Dr. Maria Rodriguez.

My research interests lie at the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, and artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on understanding how humans make decisions under uncertainty.

When not in the lab, I enjoy playing chess, reading philosophy of mind, and participating in science outreach programs that make cognitive science accessible to diverse audiences.
