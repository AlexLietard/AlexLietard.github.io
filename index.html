About Me

As a first-year Ph.D. student in Cognitive Science at KU Leuven, I work under the supervision of Kobe Desender within the COnfident DEcision european network. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology, followed by a Master's in Fundamental and Applied Cognitive Psychology, both completed at Université Paris Cité.

When you're going to a usual restaurant, there exists a clear and absolutely fundamental tension between going for meal that you know (exploitation) or trying out new taste (exploration). The goal of my research is to investigate how the feeling of confidence could be involved into the decision of this exploration-exploitation dilemma.
